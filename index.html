The Philharmonia Project

How it started

We, a group of French students passionate about classical music, realized that operas and concert halls often have special offers for young people but they are all different and you can quickly be lost, not knowing which ones to choose.

What we do

At Philharmonia, our goal is to centralize all information concerning classical concerts and especially the offers for young people. Orchestras and theatres have made great efforts to offer attractive prices for young people, it would be a shame not to take the most out of it. Concretely we work with theatres to make sure information are available and sufficiently clear and precise then we centralize it all on our app.
